# The Realm of Aetheria

## World Overview
Aetheria is a land where magic flows like water through invisible currents called the Aetherstream. The continent is divided into four regions: the Verdant Reach (fertile farmlands), the Ashen Wastes (volcanic badlands), the Sunken Depths (underground cities), and the Frostmere (northern tundra). Civilization huddles around the lifeblood rivers that channel the Aetherstream.

## Starting Location: Oakhaven
A walled village at the edge of the Verdant Reach, where the Whisperwood meets the Amberfields. Population ~300. Ruled by a council of three: Eldermayor Thorne (human), Captain Vex (half-elf garrison leader), and Sage Mira (human aether-sage). The town has a tavern (The Broken Antler), a humble temple of the Luminous Order, a market square, and an old watchtower overlooking the forest.

## Factions
- **The Iron Crown**: Human empire to the south. Expansionist, technology-focused, suspicious of wild magic. Trade outposts exist near Oakhaven.
- **The Wildwood Circle**: Druids and fey-touched who protect the ancient forests. They speak for the old spirits and distrust Iron Crown expansion. Their scouts sometimes pass through Oakhaven.
- **The Sunken Guild**: An underground network of merchants, thieves, and explorers who control trade routes through the Sunken Depths. Neutral and pragmatic. They have a small, discreet presence in every town.
- **The Luminous Order**: Monks who study the Aetherstream directly. They maintain small temples as safe havens for travelers and keepers of knowledge.

## Magic System
Four schools, each attuned to a color of the Aetherstream:
- **Ember** (red/orange): fire, heat, courage — impulsive and aggressive
- **Frost** (blue/white): ice, calm, clarity — precise and cold
- **Aether** (gold/purple): pure energy, telekinesis, light — the rarest gift
- **Shadow** (dark green/violet): stealth, illusion, nature — subtle and patient

Magic requires focus and aetherial resonance. Most people can sense the Aetherstream; fewer can channel it. Spellcasting drains stamina (HP).

## Notable NPCs (Oakhaven)
- **Sage Mira**: Elderly woman, gold-flecked eyes (Aether-aligned). She runs the temple of the Luminous Order. Wise, cryptic, always offering tea and riddles.
- **Captain Vex**: Half-elf with a scar across her left brow. Commands the small town guard (12 soldiers). Pragmatic, sharp-tongued, secretly lonely.
- **Brennus the Bard**: A wandering storyteller who stays at The Broken Antler for weeks at a time. Knows everyone's secrets but tells no one. Plays a battered lute.
- **Old Kael**: A retired Sunken Guild prospector who lives on the edge of town. His shack is full of strange relics. Talks to himself.

## Tone
Hopeful but dangerous. Combat is deadly. Magic is wondrous but carries consequences. NPCs have their own goals. The world reacts to the player's choices — reputation, faction standing, and past actions matter.
